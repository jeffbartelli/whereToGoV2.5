Where to Go?
Are you ready to move but don't know where to? Are you thinking of heading west but still considering some eastern cities? If so then you're in luck!
I created this tool to figure out which city was right for me.
Adjust the 24 metrics below based on your preferences. You can turn each on or off. You can also adjust how important a metric is to you (0-100). As you make changes the rankings will adjust accordingly.
When you select a city from the dropdown its scores and rankings will appear in the extended details of each metric.
And when you're finished there's no need to save your settings: everything will still be set the next time you come to this site. For each group of settings below you can turn individual settings on or off, and you can adjust how important each setting is for you. The rankings below will adjust as you go!
